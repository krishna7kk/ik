About Us

Founded in 1998, Sri Venkateswara Constructions is a premier electrical contractor specializing in industrial projects. With over 27 years of experience, we deliver high-quality, reliable, and safe electrical solutions tailored to our clients' needs. Our partnerships with industry leaders like Base Automation, Piramal Pharma, Mithiri Laboratories, and Aurobindo Pharma reflect our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Our Journey Since 1998

1998 - Foundation

Sri Venkateswara Constructions was established in Hyderabad, starting with small-scale electrical projects.

2005 - Piramal Pharma Ltd

Began collaboration with Piramal Pharma Ltd in Ennore, Chennai, for advanced automation projects.

2012 - Major Project with Base Automation Ltd

Completed electrical installations for Base Automation Ltd's facility in Ennore, Chennai, marking a key milestone.

2018 - Collaboration with Maithri Laboratories

Provided electrical solutions for Maithri Laboratories in Lingottam, Hyderabad, enhancing our pharmaceutical expertise.

2022 - Aurobindo Pharma Project

Executed power system upgrades for Aurobindo Pharma Ltd in Sangareddy, Hyderabad, strengthening our industry presence.

2025 - Continued Excellence

Celebrating 27 years of delivering innovative electrical solutions to leading clients across India.
